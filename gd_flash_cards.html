Contrast

Make stuff pop

What is it?

Contrast creates visual interest and draws attention by using opposing elements like light vs. dark, large vs. small, or thick vs. thin.

Example: A bold black headline on a white background, or mixing a heavy serif font with a light sans-serif font.
